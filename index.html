Definition of Word2Vec
Word2Vec takes a large corpus of text as its input and produces a vector. Each word in the corpus correspond to a vector that is different from one an200. People convert words to vectors for convenience of applying mathematical operations, e.g., you can easily subtract or add “words” in the form of vectors. Word2Vec uses shallow, two-layer neural networks trained from contexts of corpus words to create these vectors.

Where to use
Did you know that Word2Vec is not only for "Words"? We can treat "Items" as "Words" and apply similar machine learning algorithm. We've used a metaphor; movies to words, and personal movie archives to sentences. If majority of people who watched "Batman" also watched "Dark Knight", then this movie can be recommended for the newbies of "Batman". Likewise, Word2Vec can be used to recommend various items to customers.
