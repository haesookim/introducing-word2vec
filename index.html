Definition of Word2Vec
Word2Vec is a method of turning text input into a unique numerical vector. These vectors can then be used in mathematical operations, e.g., 'adding' or 'subtracting' words. These values are called Relationship Vectors.
Relationship vectors
Look at the highlighted words on the right. The distance between 'Korea' and 'Seoul' is same as that of 'Greece' and 'Athens'. Their relationship is country-capital. See another example
Where to use
Word2Vec can also be applied outside of words, in building recommendation systems. Here, we mapped movies to vectors using movie review texts. A user's preference can also be expressed as a vector, and Word2Vec can be used to recommend various items to customers.
