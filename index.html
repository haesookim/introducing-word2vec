Definition of Word2Vec
Word2Vec takes a large corpus of text as its input and produces a vector. Each word in the corpus correspond to a vector that is different from one another. People convert words to vectors for convenience of applying mathematical operations, e.g., you can easily subtract or add “words” in the form of vectors. Word2Vec uses shallow, two-layer neural networks trained from contexts of corpus words to create these vectors.
