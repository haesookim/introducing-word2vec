Introducing Word2Vec

Are you a recommendation designer who needs to understand how embedding models work? A student lost in your first NLP lecture? A curious enthusiast? Whichever you are, we can help. In this module, we will learn about the concept of Word2Vec as a word-embedding model, as well as look at the ways that Word2Vec could be applied to the real world.
1. What is Word2Vec?
Word2Vec is a method of producing word-embeddings that involve training a large corpus of text data and transforming it into a set of vectors. That's right - vectors as in the ones you see in coordinates and spaces. How do these apply to text and words, a distinctly abstract form of information?
